Product Information Management (PIM)

PIM solutions aim to create a single view of a product across a (retailer's) enterprise - the idea of Master Data Management (MDM) applied to the Product Information domain.

Typically, a PIM system would assist with aggregation and standardization of product data from heterogeneous sources, classifying and relating products and cross-channel distribution of this information.
